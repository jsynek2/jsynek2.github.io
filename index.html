Who is Bubbie?

About About

Bubbie (aka Rengar) is my cat. I have had him for around 2 years now and he makes my life better. He like to flop in my arms when I hold him and he knows how to play fetch. He loves to follow me around the house and likes to be around people. He is treated like a prince at my parents house, and although he loves my family, him and I have a special bond.
